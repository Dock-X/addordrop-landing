Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. We keep it simple — we only collect what we need to run the service.

1. Who We Are

AddOrDrop.com is operated by Blank Studio LLC, a Tennessee limited liability company. References to "we," "us," or "our" in this policy refer to Blank Studio LLC.

Questions? Contact us at [email protected]

2. What We Collect

Data Why We Collect It
Email address Account verification, free trial tracking, Pro access management, transactional emails
Device fingerprint Free trial fraud prevention — prevents abuse of one free trial per tool
Usage data (which tools used, trial status) Managing your account and free trial limits per season
Payment information Processed entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your card details
Fantasy football data you input Processed for analysis — not stored after the session
Roster screenshots (if uploaded) Processed for text extraction then immediately discarded — not stored

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, or any sensitive personal information.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To verify your identity and manage your account
  • To track free trial usage and Pro access per season
  • To process payments via Stripe
  • To send verification codes and essential account emails
  • To prevent abuse and protect the integrity of the service

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as described in Section 4.

4. Third Parties

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate AddOrDrop.com:

  • Supabase — database hosting for user accounts and trial tracking
  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Anthropic — analysis engine. Your fantasy football inputs are sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic's privacy policy applies.
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (verification codes)
  • Vercel — hosting and infrastructure

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy governing their use of data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your email address and account data for as long as you have an account with us. Fantasy football inputs and roster screenshots are not stored after your session ends.

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing [email protected]. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

AddOrDrop.com uses browser local storage (not traditional cookies) to remember your Pro status and preferences between sessions. No tracking cookies or advertising pixels are used.

You can clear local storage at any time through your browser settings. This will log you out of any active session.

7. Children's Privacy

AddOrDrop.com is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), secure database hosting via Supabase, and no storage of payment card details. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
